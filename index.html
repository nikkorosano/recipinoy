Famous Dishes

Here are some of the most searched recipes here at ReciPinoy

Pork Adobo expand_more
See Recipe
Pork Adobo close

It is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. There are version wherein onions are also added. Adobo is a popular dish in the Philippines, along with Sinigang. Adobo, in general, can be cooked using different kinds of protein.

Chicken Afritada expand_more
See recipe
Chicken Afritada close

Chicken Afritada or afritadang manok is a type of Filipino chicken stew with tomato based sauced. It is a simple dish that can be cooked on regular days. It is perfect for lunch and is better when eaten with warm white rice.

Sizzling Chicken Sisig expand_more
See recipe
Sizzling Chicken Sisig close

Sizzling Chicken Sisig is the chicken version of the popular sisig dish. This recipe is quick and easy and can be low in fat depending on the type and amount of mayonnaise that will be used.

Beef Caldereta expand_more
See recipe
Beef Caldereta close

With meat cooked until so tender it falls off the bone, it’s no secret that beef caldereta is a Filipino favorite. A mix of ingredients come together in this tomato-based stew practically bursting with flavor. The beef caldereta recipe is one you’ll find in practically any Filipino cookbook
